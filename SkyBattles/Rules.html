Sky Battles - Official Game Rules

1. Cheating / Exploiting
Using cheats, exploits, dnSpy, JavaScript executors, or any unauthorized software is strictly forbidden. Violators will receive a permanent ban and device ban.
2. Sexual Content
Any sexual or inappropriate language, such as terms like “sex” or “ass”, is not allowed. This will result in a 1-month.
3. Respect Others
Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or toxic behavior will not be tolerated. Treat everyone with respect or face warnings, mutes, or bans.
4. Follow Staff Instructions
All players must follow instructions from moderators and administrators. Arguing with staff may result in disciplinary action.
5. Account Security
You are responsible for your own account. Do not share it. Lost or stolen accounts are your responsibility.
6. No Account Trading
Buying, selling, or trading accounts is strictly prohibited. Violators may be permanently banned.
7. Economy Exploits
Attempting to duplicate, exploit, or manipulate the in-game economy is forbidden. This will lead to a permanent ban.
8. Purchases Are Final
All purchases of virtual currency or items are final and non-refundable.
9. No Real-World Value
Sky Battles currency and items have no real-world value. Trying to exchange them for real money or services is not allowed.
10. Game Updates
Rules and features may change. By continuing to play, you agree to follow the latest rules and game updates.
